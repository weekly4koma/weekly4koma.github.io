Subject: [w4k] - Weekly 4-koma Thread

Comment:

A new weekly exercise/challenge for all anons of all levels.

Since this is the first thread, you can pick any theme, but in the future, 4 themes will be posted per thread and posters will be expected to pick one of them.

Today's themes are:
> Accident
> Hole
> Losing Control
> Hunger

Although 4-koma are mostly gag comic strips, feel free to test waters and try different genres.

If successful, this and every following thread will be archived. If you wish to link your socials for the archive, feel free to do so.

===

>What's the goal?
Initially my goal here is to consistently provide a simple looking exercise to myself and other anons who aren't feeling very motivated. I strongly believe it's better to produce something simple rather than nothing, and 4-koma's format allows for a lot of experimentation.

In the future, I hope to experiment with other formats beyond 4-koma and maybe even organize an internal /ic/ contest, but it will of course depend on how these threads perform.

===

>What is a 4-koma?
It is a comic strip of four panels of equal size which normally uses the following format: Context / Goal / Conflict / Resolution.
